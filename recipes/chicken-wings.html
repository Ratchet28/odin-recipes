Chicken Wings

Description

Quick and easy crispy chicken wings using an air fryer!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs. chicken wings
  • Buffulo sauce
  • Green onions
  • Super secret mix of seasonings

Steps

  1. Dry rub super secret mix of seasonings onto chicken wings
  2. Cook chicken wings in air fryer for 18 minutes at 380
  3. Coat chicken wings in buffulo sauce to your liking
  4. Garnish with green onions and serve!
