Caesar Salad

Description

Crisp lettuce, crunchy croutons and a simple caesar dressing! You end up with an extremly simple but tasty salad!

Ingredients

  • Fresh Romain Lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Bacon Bits
  • Parmeson Cheese
  • Caesar Dressing

Steps

  1. Pull romain lettuce into bit sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl mix lettuce, croutons and bacon bits together.
  3. Mix in caesar dressing to your liking
  4. Shred parmeson onto salad and serve
