Tacos

Description

Good old fashion tacos. Simple and easy to make. Can use your choice of flour or corn tortilla's and customize the topping as you like!

Ingredtients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in frying pan.
  2. Cut up onions and lettuce.
  3. Grade cheese and warm up torillas
  4. Put all Ingredtients on tortilla and enjoy
